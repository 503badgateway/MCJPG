# Last Login: Sun Jun
Sun Jun, at 28 years old, has left us. The next reunion will be in the early morning hours 11 years from now.  
As **MCJPG** celebrates its 1.8th birthday, we once again make our voice heard.  
In the collision of keys and code, all remains the same, yet everything is renewed.  
Below, we bring you a report of our efforts and achievements in June.  
We are grateful to all those who have quietly dedicated themselves and contributed.

## Updates
### ServerEditor
We upgraded the official website's server list backend from static hosted JSON to a dynamic backend.  
This upgrade allows server owners to independently submit, edit, and browse the list on the page, without having to log into Github to submit a PR as before.  
[↘Visit ServerEditor](https://server-editor.mcjpg.dev)  
  
The project is now fully open-sourced. Participation is welcome — submit your code, issues, or any reasonable improvement suggestions.  
[↘GitHub:MineJPGcraft/ServerEditor](https://github.com/MineJPGcraft/ServerEditor)

### Developer Platform
To optimize the community interconnection ecosystem, we have officially released a brand new open platform.  
Here, server owners can quickly integrate their independent applications, community pages, or game servers with MCJPG Passport login;  
The platform also provides free subdomains, making it easy for servers without a domain to quickly get connected and lowering the operational barrier.  
[↘Visit the Developer Platform](https://dash.mcjpg.dev/)

### CharityAPI
We provide server owners with non-profit large model API services, facilitating the construction of AI applications for community and game interaction.  
Such as: knowledge base Q&A bots, game troubleshooting assistance, etc.  
[↘Visit CharityAPI](https://charity-api.mcjpg.dev)

## Note
The above applications require the use of an MCJPG **Developer** Passport to log in, which is not compatible with the MCJPG **Community** Passport.  
The MCJPG **Developer** Passport requires server owners to independently apply to the organization for registration.

## Conclusion
Time flies, and the months pass quickly. May this update serve as a coming-of-age milestone for **MCJPG**, as she approaches her 2nd birthday. We sincerely wish:  
Thank you for being with us on this journey; looking ahead, until we meet again.
